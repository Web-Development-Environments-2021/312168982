About Me

My name is Hadasa Ulner and I am 85 years old.
I was born at Viena, Austria. Because of WW2, My family and I ran away from Europe and moved to Argentina, where I met my husband Israel.
We had a big company of clothes shop, and we came to Israel at 1967. My husband died at 2015, and I live alone since then.
The corona virus came and forced me to stay home, alone, without my family.
